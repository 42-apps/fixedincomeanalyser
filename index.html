How "Best overall" is scored

Each instrument gets a Quality Score (0–100) blending four things. Yield is normalised within each currency — so a 9% rand bond and a 4% dollar bond are each judged against their own market, not each other. Drag the weights to match your priorities — e.g. push Reputation & Safety up and a flashy-but-shaky 11.5% (STRC) falls below a rock-solid 6.25% (Alphabet). Weights re-normalise to 100%.
